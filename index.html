President's Welcome


Welcome to the official website of Kappa Theta Pi - Nu Chapter at the University of Colorado at Boulder! If this is your first time hearing about us – we’re a professional technology fraternity on campus, driven by a mission to secure tech jobs for all our members. Joining KTP means gaining access to various resources designed to help you succeed in the tech industry. We offer a variety of workshops, academic support, community events, and hands-on projects to launch your career. You’ll immerse yourself in a community of peers who share your passion whatever that passion may be! We pride ourselves in our diverse community with interests spanning from quantitative finance to data science to biomedical engineering.

We’re always looking to grow our membership! So many industries work closely with technology and there is no better time to get involved than today. Whether your passion lies in software engineering, design, or any other tech-related field, there’s a place for you at KTP. I encourage you to explore our website and Instagram to get to know our organization better. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us through a direct message or email, or arrange a coffee chat with any of our members. We want you to be part of our story and help us shape the future. I look forward to seeing you at our recruiting events!

Wishing you nothing but the best and a warm welcome to KTP!

Langston Denning
President, Spring 2025

Pillars

Professional Development

KTP helps members build professional skills through interview training, resume building, one-on-one mentorship, private company recruiting, and more. We aim to prepare members for success in any technology-related career.

Alumni Connections

KTP has a vast alumni network, filled with people working all over the globe. From tech companies like Microsoft, Amazon, Facebook, Apple, and Google, to startups, consulting firms, and financial technology firms, this network has it all.

Social Growth

KTP is not just a professional fraternity, but also makes social connections a priority. We build and nuture these friendships through events including tailgates, bowling, retreats, and KTP formal.

Technical Advancement

We prioritize creating and expanding our members' technical skillsets. Whether it's by participating in one of our project teams or attending a workshop, there is always something to learn.

Academic Support

KTP Boulder is made up of some of the brightest minds across CU's campus. This vast network is there to provide help both inside and outside of the classroom.
